Bike Tours

Try exploring Bozeman, Montana and the surrounding area on a bike.

Cycle Bozeman

Overview

Experience spectacular mountain ranges and scenic valleys.

Details

The tour will start in Bozeman. We’ll climb through the Tobacco Root Mountains and on to Virginia City and Nevada City. You will follow the Jefferson River into the town of Dillon. We’ll parallel the Big Hole River before ascending to the Continental Divide and stunning 360-degree views. Moving on into the legendary copper mining city of Butte and toward the headwaters of the Missouri River. Then back to Bozeman.

Length: 7 days, about 350 miles
Skill Level: Intermediate+
Cost: $1,499.00
Location: Bozeman, Montana

1/2 Day Mountain Bike Tours

Overview

Come spend half a day enjoying the beautiful area around Bozeman. Will start at the stroe, head through historic downtown. Then will hop onto a a trail that will take us around some scenic views. There will be time for pictures and breaks.

Hire a Guide

If you don't want to be alone and want someone to tell you about Bozeman as you ride and break. Hire a Guide, cost is only $10 more.

Length: 4.5 hours, about 18 miles
Skill Level: Beginner to intermediate
Cost: $160 per person or $130 per person with 2 or more people
Location: Bozeman, Montana
